How to play Rock Paper Scissors?

Rock beats scissors and loses to paper.
To throw a stone, select the clenched fist buttons on the left side of the game window

Paper beats rock, but loses to scissors.
To throw the paper, select the open hand buttons in the center of the game window

Scissors beat paper but loses to rock.
To throw scissors, select the button with a clenched fist and two fingers extended on the right side of the game window
